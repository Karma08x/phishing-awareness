Phishing Alert: You Have Been Phished

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to steal your personal information, often through deceptive emails, websites, or messages. The goal is to trick you into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card numbers.

If you think you may have been a victim of phishing, it's important to act quickly!

How to Stay Safe from Phishing

  • Always check the URL: Make sure the website you're on is legitimate.
  • Don't click on suspicious links: Hover over links to verify their destination.
  • Enable two-factor authentication: It adds an extra layer of security.
  • Verify emails and messages: Be cautious with unsolicited messages, especially those asking for sensitive information.
  • Update your passwords regularly: Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
